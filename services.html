Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?
You can book a service through our website by selecting the service you need, choosing your preferred date and time, and completing the booking form. Alternatively, you can call our customer service team for assistance.
Are your service providers verified?
Yes, all our service providers undergo a rigorous verification process including background checks, skill assessments, and reference verification to ensure your safety and satisfaction.
What if I need to reschedule or cancel?
You can reschedule or cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled service time at no charge. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee.
Do you offer warranties on your services?
Yes, we offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all our services. If you're not happy with the results, we'll send a professional to fix the issue at no additional cost.
