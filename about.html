We're Changing the Way You Care for Your Home

HomeServe was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to make home maintenance hassle-free for everyone. We noticed that finding reliable, skilled professionals for home services was often a time-consuming and frustrating process.

Today, we've grown into a trusted platform connecting thousands of homeowners with verified service professionals across the country. Our rigorous vetting process ensures that only the most qualified and reliable professionals join our network.

We're committed to delivering exceptional service experiences, backed by our satisfaction guarantee. When you book with HomeServe, you can rest easy knowing your home is in good hands.
